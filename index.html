Helping children with autism thrive

We're committed to making a positive impact on the lives of children diagnosed with autism spectrum disorder around the world.

Harmony Spectrum Foundation was founded with a clear goal helping children diagnosed with ASD and their families to thrive wherever they are. We believe children with ASD can live a satisfying life when suited with the right rehabilitative program.

Care & therapy

Dedicated daycare

One of our core programs. We offer daycare services to families with ASD children, so parents can go to work.
Daycare staff are all volunteers with special training.


Playtime volunteers

Playtime is one of the most import experiences children with ASD can have. It puts them at ease and increases their possibility of having positive interaction


Art therapy class

Much has been said about art as a form of therapy - we couldn't agree more. Another core program of our organization, this class helps children with ASD to open up to new people and experiences.


Join our volunteer community

Join our growing community of over 250 volunteers across the world, and make an impact in the lives of children with ASD and their families. There are numerous ways to help out.

Support a child

With as little as US $5/month, you can make a great impact on a child's life. Your donation will help a child with ASD stay in daycare, receive personal care, food, and enjoy art therapy classes.

Support a program

When you support a program, you support 25 children with ASD and their families. Your donations will help our programs run smoothly, and continue to ofer rehabilitative help to those who need it
